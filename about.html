What we do

The Globus LLC largest co-operative and a rapidly growing company in the Russian grain industry, with operations extending along the value chain from grain storage, handling, transport, marketing and processing.

Returning value to growers since 2007

Cooperating by approximately 550 Russian grain growing businesses and Collective farms, the core purpose of Globus LLC is to sustainably create and return value to Russian grain growers, current and future.

Collective farms are organisations that are owned, controlled and used by their members. They exist to deliver benefits to their members and are based on the values of self-help, self-responsibility, democracy, equality, equity and solidarity.

Our storage and handling system currently receives and exports around 12 per cent of the South West Russia grain harvest and is regarded as one of the best in the region.

The Globus LLC consists of two core business activities: Operations (storage and handling), Marketing and Trading. In Operations, Globus has a network of more than 5 grain receival sites across South West Russia and access to four export port terminals. We own a state-of-the-art rail fleet dedicated to the most efficient transfer of grain from our receival sites to the port terminals.



The co-operative’s Marketing and Trading arm is the leading grain acquirer in SWR, Russia’s emerging grain exporter, and has operations in Western Russia , as well as offices in Dubai and Baku.


Globus LLC also has investments across the grain value chain.
