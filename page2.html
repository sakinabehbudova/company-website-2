Sustainable
grain

Providing for an increasing demand in sustainable food products

Consumers around the world are more aware of the origins of their food, and there is increasing demand for sustainably produced food products, including products made from grain. We are well placed to meet this demand with Russia’s ability to produce sustainably grown and internationally accredited grain.


Our Marketing and Trading team is working closely with our customers to find opportunities to secure new markets to export sustainable grain. We certify our sustainable grain as part of the Russian Grain Union (RGU) The RGU program is an internationally recognised sustainability accreditation system. Certifications like RGU are increasingly becoming a prerequisite for sustainable sourcing strategies of major multinational food manufacturing companies.

Over the last 5 years through the RGU certification, over 10 million tonnes of South West Russian sustainable-accredited grain have been exported to the European and Asian markets.
