Grain storage

In an increasingly competitive environment, Globus aims to ensure that our grain growers and marketers have the most efficient and cost-effective supply chain from South West Russia.

Our storage services

We seek to provide growers and traders with choice and flexibility in how they would like to use the supply chain, as well as the opportunity to access lower cost pathways to port. The Globus supply chain offers key services to you.

GLOBUS Integrated

A fully integrated service managed by Globus from receival point to export or domestic market. Gobus Integrated is available at all sites in the Russian Black Sea region network ensuring growers can access buyers at every site.

Key features:
  • Available all year round
  • Full access to quality optimisation prior to cut off dates
  • Full weighing, sampling and grading of grain deliveries
